Services We Offer

Architecture

We transform visions and space into structures and environments that engage the senses, stimulate productivity and inspire the spirit of all who enter. Working closely with our clients, we create architectural statements that are equal parts personal and functional.

Interior Design

We design interior environments that enhance, enable and empower—building from the inside out to best accommodate and facilitate our clients’ requirements. The look, the feel, the flow of our designs—all are a result of our thorough understanding of our clients’ culture and expectations.

Property valuation

We value your property as if it was our own and no one really wants to deflate their price. In Naxalaya, we provide the best property valuation for both the buyers and the sellers.

Rate analysis & Cost Estimation

We really consider the time value of money in the business. After all, a dollar in hand today is worth more than a year later. So, we have best rate analysts who can have acute cost estimation for your valuable property today and tomorrow.

Survey

Survey is the most important procedure in any businesses and housing or building are likely to be once in a life time deal. Keeping this in mind, the team in Naxalaya has the best survey services and provide the result as it is like any other professionals would do.

Planning & Land Pooling

Naxalaya gives you the services of planning and land pooling as well. There will be plans for basic housing needs like water supply, electric supply, roads, drainage, adequate sun light etc. Also, land pooling will have minimum impact on the environment.
